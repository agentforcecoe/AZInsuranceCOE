Who We Are

Protecting Families Since 1998

AZ Insurance was founded in 1998 in Singapore with a singular mission: to make world-class insurance protection accessible to every family in Southeast Asia. What began as a boutique life insurance firm with a team of 40 has grown into a regional powerhouse with over 3,200 employees, 500,000 active policyholders, and dual regulatory licences in Singapore and the Philippines.

Our Singapore operations are fully regulated and supervised by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS), while our Philippine entity — AZ Insurance Corporation — holds a Certificate of Authority from the Insurance Commission (IC) and is a registered PDIC member institution. We maintain an AA credit rating from Fitch Ratings, reflecting our long-term financial strength.

At AZ Insurance, we believe that insurance is ultimately a promise — a commitment to be there when life takes an unexpected turn. Every product we build, every policy we issue, and every claim we settle is guided by that core belief.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

From a small Singapore office to a regional insurance group — our story, year by year.

1998

Founded in Singapore

AZ Insurance Ltd. incorporated in Singapore with 40 employees and a founding capital of SGD 50 million. First product: AZ Legacy Life Assurance.

2003

MAS Full Insurance Licence

Awarded full composite insurance licence by the Monetary Authority of Singapore, enabling both life and general insurance operations under one entity.

2008

Philippines Expansion

AZ Insurance Philippines Inc. established in Makati City, obtaining a Certificate of Authority from the Insurance Commission of the Philippines.

2013

100,000 Policyholders

Crossed the 100,000 active policyholders milestone. Launched AZ Maxilink Prime VUL — the company's first variable universal life product in the Philippines.

2017

Bancassurance Partnerships

Signed exclusive bancassurance distribution agreements with BPI and Metrobank in the Philippines, significantly expanding retail distribution reach.

2020

MyAZ Digital Platform Launch

Launched the MyAZ mobile app enabling digital policy management, online claims submission, and 24/7 customer support for 250,000+ users.

2024

AA Credit Rating by Fitch

Independently rated AA by Fitch Ratings — the first Singapore-Philippines dual-jurisdiction insurer to achieve this rating, reflecting exceptional financial strength.

2026

500,000 Policyholders & 20-Product Portfolio

AZ Insurance reaches 500,000 active policyholders across both markets with a full 20-product portfolio spanning life, health, motor, travel, home, and investment.
