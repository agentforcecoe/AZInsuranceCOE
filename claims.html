How to File

Simple 4-Step Claims Process

From notification to disbursement, we keep you informed at every stage.

1

Notify AZ Insurance

Call our 24/7 claims hotline (+65 6222 0000 / +63 2 8888 0000) or submit online via MyAZ portal within 30 days of the event.

2

Submit Documents

Upload or courier required documents. Your claims manager will send a personalised checklist within 1 business day of notification.

3

Assessment

Our experienced claims assessors review your submission. We may request a medical examination or specialist report for certain claims.

4

Disbursement

Approved claim amounts are disbursed via bank transfer within 5 business days. Death claims include a bereavement support call.

Documentation Guide

What You'll Need

Prepare the right documents to ensure your claim is processed without delay.

🛡️ Life & TPD Claims

  • ✓ Completed AZ Claim Form (Form LC-01)
  • ✓ Original Policy Document or Policy Number
  • ✓ Certified Death Certificate (for death claims)
  • ✓ Medical Reports & Specialist Certification (for TPD)
  • ✓ NRIC / Passport of Claimant & Beneficiary
  • ✓ Bank Account Details for Disbursement

❤️ Health & Medical Claims

  • ✓ Hospital Discharge Summary
  • ✓ Itemised Medical Bills & Receipts
  • ✓ Doctor's Referral Letters & Lab Reports
  • ✓ Completed AZ Claim Form (Form HC-02)
  • ✓ Insurance Guarantee Letter (if used)

🚗 Motor & General Claims

  • ✓ Police Report (if accident or theft)
  • ✓ Completed Vehicle Accident Form
  • ✓ Photos of Damage
  • ✓ Driver's Licence & Vehicle Registration
  • ✓ Repair Estimates from Authorised Workshop

✈️ Travel Claims

  • ✓ Airline Boarding Passes (proof of travel)
  • ✓ Official Medical Receipts (overseas treatment)
  • ✓ Airline Baggage Irregularity Report
  • ✓ Trip Cancellation Evidence (e.g. death cert, hospital admission)
  • ✓ Completed AZ Travel Claim Form (Form TC-04)

🏠 Home & Property Claims

  • ✓ Fire Marshal or Police Report
  • ✓ Photographs of Structural & Contents Damage
  • ✓ Repair / Replacement Quotations
  • ✓ Proof of Ownership (title deed or tenancy agreement)
  • ✓ Completed AZ Property Claim Form (Form PC-05)

🩺 Critical Illness & Cancer Claims

  • ✓ Histopathology / Biopsy Report
  • ✓ Oncologist / Specialist Diagnosis Letter
  • ✓ Hospital Admission & Discharge Records
  • ✓ Completed AZ CI Claim Form (Form CI-06)
  • ✓ NRIC / Passport of Claimant
